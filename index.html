Daenerys Targaryen - Mother of Dragons

Princess Daenerys Targaryan was one of the last confirmed members of House Targaryen. She spent her life in exile. Sold, butchered, raped, shamed, betrayed, defiled; but she rose from ashes, conquered her dreams, freed millions from slavery and fought for justice.

The character was written by 'George R.R. Martin' and featured in 'The Song of Ice and Fire' and 'Game Of Thrones', which was also adapted into a television series by HBO by the same name, with Daenerys being played by actress Emilia Clarke.
